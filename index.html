    What are Brand Semantics?

    Brand Semantics is the specialist practice of brand datafication — in other words, structuring a brand’s meaning as datasets that can be recognised, interpreted, and acted on by AI systems. As brands compete in an increasingly intelligent and automated world, Brand Semantics helps ensure every component of a brand becomes machine-readable, discoverable, and actionable across AI platforms.

    Why Multimodal AI Systems are changing everything

    The next era of artificial intelligence is defined by multimodal AI systems—AI that can understand and work with multiple types of information, or modalities, either sequentially or simultaneously. Where traditional AI excels at text or images or audio, multimodal AI agents can process all of these together, building a far richer, more comprehensive understanding.

    Think of it like this:

    A person with only hearing can follow a conversation, one with only vision can read body language, but someone with all senses gets the complete picture. That’s the power of multimodal AI.

    What makes this truly revolutionary is when multimodality is combined with agency: the ability to act independently towards goals. These systems don’t just interpret diverse brand signals, they take meaningful action based on them. This is already transforming customer service, content creation, marketing, and operations.

    Brands that prepare their data and identity for these multimodal agents will lead in the decade ahead. Brand Semantics is your foundation for this transformation.

    What is Brando Schema and why is it so important?

    Brando® Schema is a toolkit developed by Advanced Analytica for operationalising brands for multimodal AI system interaction, empowering organisations to encode every element of their brand into AI-optimised datasets. Brando Schema bridges the gap between human brand meaning and artificial intelligence, providing brands with the essential data layer for AI-driven brand governance, compliance, innovation, and operation.

    Brando Schema is an open-source, machine-readable schema for brand identity, enabling brands to be interoperable, enforceable, and discoverable across AI systems, intelligent agents, and large language models.

    Extending the foundational schema:Brand, Brando modularises brand identity into six core concepts: Verbal Identity, Visual Identity, Audio Identity, Positioning, Governance, and Assets.

    Brando enables consistent; compliant; and AI-aligned brand expression, whether you're designing for multimodal AI systems, autonomous brand agents, or intelligent search platforms.
