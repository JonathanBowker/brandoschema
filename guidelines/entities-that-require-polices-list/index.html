    MASTER LIST: ALL ENTITIES THAT REQUIRE POLICIES

    (For Brando or any enterprise brand)

    These are grouped into 10 governance verticals, each containing dozens of policy-bound entities. This is the most complete list possible.

    1. Brand Identity Entities

    Policies that govern the brand itself.

    1.1 Brand-Level Policies

    • Brand identity
    • Brand tone
    • Brand vocabulary
    • Brand personality
    • Brand structure and naming
    • Tagline and claims
    • Brand usage rules
    • Brand refusal behaviors
    • Semantic narrative rules

    1.2 Sub-Brand Policies

    • Sub-brand tone
    • Sub-brand vocabulary
    • Sub-brand inheritance rules
    • Sub-brand separation boundaries
    • Co-brand rules

    2. Contextual Entities

    Policies tied to where and how the brand appears.

    2.1 Channel Contexts

    • Website
    • Email
    • SMS
    • Paid Social
    • Organic Social
    • Print
    • App UI
    • Agent/Chatbot responses
    • Retail signage
    • Packaging

    2.2 Audience Contexts

    • Consumer
    • B2B
    • Executive
    • Developer
    • Investor
    • Regional audiences

    2.3 Regional / Regulatory Contexts

    • US
    • UK
    • EU
    • APAC
    • Middle East
    • LATAM

    Each region may require:

    • tone variations
    • compliance adjustments
    • claim restrictions

    3. Content Entities

    Policies that govern generated output.

    3.1 Tone & Language

    • sentence structure
    • allowed vocabulary
    • banned vocabulary
    • tone boundaries
    • grammar rules

    3.2 Style & Structure

    • paragraph length
    • metadata rules
    • formatting rules
    • disclaimers
    • refusal patterns
    • clarity requirements

    3.3 Brand Messaging

    • value pillars
    • product messaging
    • campaign messages
    • semantic narrative patterns

    4. Product & Commerce Entities

    Policies that govern products, categories, and commerce operations.

    4.1 Product-Level Policies

    • product claims
    • product descriptions
    • product limitations
    • compliance boundaries
    • safety constraints

    4.2 Category-Level Policies

    • category messaging
    • claim restrictions by category
    • safety restrictions
    • legal disclaimers

    4.3 GTIN / SKU Policies

    • SKU-specific rules
    • GTIN-level restrictions
    • variation rules
    • regional SKU rules

    4.4 Pricing / Comparison Policies

    • allowed comparative statements
    • prohibited comparisons
    • promotional constraints

    5. Visual & Asset Entities

    Policies governing brand visuals and asset usage.

    5.1 Logo Policies

    • usage rules
    • minimum size
    • color restrictions
    • background constraints

    5.2 Imagery Policies

    • approved image types
    • restricted imagery
    • generative AI image rules
    • composition rules

    5.3 Icon / Token Policies

    • style
    • line weights
    • semantic meaning

    5.4 Multimedia Policies

    • audio tone
    • video pacing
    • 3D representation rules

    6. AI Behavior Entities

    Policies that govern how LLMs and agents behave.

    6.1 Agent Decision Policies

    • allowed actions
    • prohibited actions
    • escalation rules
    • confirmation requirements
    • memory boundaries

    6.2 Multi-Step Dialogue Policies

    • tone consistency rules
    • interaction boundaries
    • conversation flow constraints

    6.3 Prompting Policies

    • identity loading
    • constraints
    • vocabulary rules
    • audit requirements

    6.4 Autonomous System Policies

    • workflow restrictions
    • sequential validation
    • override protocols

    7. Safety & Compliance Entities

    Policies governing risk, legal compliance, and regulated content.

    7.1 Risk-Level Policies

    • identity drift
    • off-brand output
    • regulatory risk
    • high-risk content

    7.2 Sector-Specific Policies

    • healthcare
    • finance
    • insurance
    • alcohol & tobacco
    • kids/education
    • consumer claims

    7.3 Claim Policies

    • supported claims
    • prohibited claims
    • conditional claims
    • time-based claims

    7.4 Legal Red-Line Policies

    • disclaimers
    • privacy statements
    • terms-of-use boundaries

    8. Campaign & Temporary Entities

    Policies that apply temporarily.

    8.1 Campaign Overrides

    • campaign tone
    • promotional messaging
    • visual overrides
    • product pushes

    8.2 Seasonal Policies

    • holiday rules
    • region-specific windows

    8.3 Time-Bound Compliance Changes

    • new regulations
    • policy shifts
    • temporary disclaimers

    9. Data & Privacy Entities

    Policies governing data in AI workflows.

    9.1 Data Handling

    • PII boundaries
    • retention policies
    • privacy modes
    • agent memory restrictions

    9.2 Retrieval / RAG Policies

    • allowed sources
    • prohibited sources
    • rewriting rules
    • tone alignment rules
    • claim verification

    9.3 Log & Trace Policies

    • audit logs
    • safety logging
    • incident response

    10. Partnership & Ecosystem Entities

    Policies governing partners, integrators, agencies, and third parties.

    10.1 Partner Identity Policies

    • co-branding
    • asset permissions
    • naming conventions

    10.2 Agency Policies

    • creative constraints
    • vocabulary restrictions
    • approval workflows

    10.3 Integrator / Platform Policies

    • embedding BrandoSchema
    • API usage
    • tone governance
    • asset injection rules

    10.4 White-Label Policies

    • identity separation
    • claim restrictions
    • tone neutralization
